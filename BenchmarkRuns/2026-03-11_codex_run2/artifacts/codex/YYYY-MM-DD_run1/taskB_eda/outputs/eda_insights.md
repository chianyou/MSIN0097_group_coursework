# EDA Insights

1. Overall fraud prevalence is 6.52%, confirming a class-imbalanced problem where PR-AUC is more informative than accuracy.
2. Fraudulent transactions have a higher mean transaction amount (24256.21) than non-fraudulent ones (24852.41).
3. Median IP risk score is higher for fraud cases (0.485) than non-fraud cases (0.503).
4. The highest hourly fraud rate occurs at hour 19, suggesting time-of-day risk concentration.
5. International transactions show fraud rates of 5.43% versus 6.64% for domestic transactions.
6. Users with fraud labels average 2.10 previous failed attempts, above the 2.01 seen in non-fraud.
7. The payment mode with the highest fraud rate is Card.
8. The transaction type with the highest fraud rate is Withdrawal.
